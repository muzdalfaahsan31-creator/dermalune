Portable Skincare Capsules for the Modern Traveler.
🌟 Overview
Dermalune provides high-quality skincare in a compact, capsule format. Designed for people on the go, these capsules ensure your skin stays radiant without the bulk of traditional bottles.
✨ Key Features
• Easy to Carry: Fits perfectly in small travel bags or purses.
• Single-Dose Precision: No more guessing how much product to use.
• Travel-Friendly: Leak-proof and TSA-compliant.
🧴  How to Use
1.	Twist: Gently twist the neck of the capsule to open.
2.	Apply: Squeeze the contents onto your fingertips.
3.	Massage: Smooth over clean face and neck until fully absorbed.
